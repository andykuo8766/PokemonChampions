# Pokémon Champions Meta - April 2026

## 🛠️ Major System Changes
- **Removal of IVs**: Individual Values are completely removed. Customization is now handled via **Stat Sliders** (EVs) and Nature selection.
- **Protect Nerf**: The most significant change. Max PP for Protect has been slashed from **16 $\rightarrow$ 8**, discouraging stall and rewarding precise reads.
- **Other PP Nerfs**: 
  - Earthquake: Max 12 PP (was 16)
  - Hydro Pump: Max 8 PP
  - Recover/Roost: Significantly limited.
- **Stat Drop Calculation**: A -1 Attack drop now results in a **0.7x multiplier** (instead of 0.66x), making Intimidate slightly less oppressive.

## 💡 Key Meta Insights
- **Mega Evolutions**: The meta is currently defined by **Mega Froslass** (Snow/Blizzard) and **Mega Dragonite** (Bulk/Sweeper).
- **Incineroar**: Still viable but has **lost access to Knock Off**, significantly reducing its item-disruption capabilities.
- **Trick Room**: Harder to execute without 0 Speed IVs. Trainers now rely on naturally low base speed and items like Iron Ball.
